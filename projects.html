Minesweeper in Java

For my CIS 120 final project, I created a Minesweeper game. Functionalities included the ability to uncover sqaures, flag and unflag, save progress, see remaining flags with a live progress bar, "always safe first click", and restart. I also drew out the flags and squares within Java Swing.

Bubble Tea Analysis

In my junior year of high school, I decided on a whim to start jotting down every instance of a bubble tea purchase in my notes app. On one particularly uneventful summer two and a half years later, I noticed that my notes app had gotten considerably large (86 entries, with the inclusion of date, store, type of tea, toppings, and ice level ). An idea occured to experiment with the data and see what I could uncover about my bubble tea consumption habits.
