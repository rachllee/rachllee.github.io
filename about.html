Summary

My name is Rachel Lee, and I'm a rising undergraduate sophomore at the University of Pennsylvania. At Penn, I'm enrolled in the Wharton school, with concentrations in Finance and Management, and a minor in Computer and Information Science.

Although I first applied and matriculated into Wharton as pure business, two courses I took in my freshman year (CIS 1200 & CIS 1600) led me to realize my interests in computer science. I'm now taking the steps to pursue an accelerated masters in CIS, which I'll be applying for at the end of my sophomore year.

In my free time, I enjoy reading (book section of website coming soon!!), biking, traveling, quanitfying random aspects of my life (see in projects), film photography, and recently, DJ'ing!
